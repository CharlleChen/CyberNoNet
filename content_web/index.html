Cybersecurity for all

Cybersecurity is about the security of data. It is around us everywhere nowadays as technological devices become ubiquitous. But do you know that there is a huge supply gap in the cybersecurity industry? According to cyberseek.org, there are only enough workers for 68% of the available cybersecurity jobs.

Cybersecurity Engineer Bootcamp

We are dedicated to helping you enter this industry with quick yet comprehensive tutorials with NO COST except your own effort! We will equip you with cybersecurity skills and become a cybersecurity engineer, who guards data security.

Introduction to cybersecurity

Do you know what is asymmetric encryption and how it enables authentication? Do you know the underlying technology of Bitcoin? In this very first class, we will draw a blueprint of cybersecurity for you and go over some essential skills you need to become a qualified cybersecurity engineer.

Features

The courses are designed in a learner-friendly way that introduces the concepts at a slow pace and provides customizable resources for students at different levels.

Bootstrap 4

The whole course is carefully designed and divided into 4 pieces. You can learn each module with interesting animations and approachable lecture videos.

Customizable

You can access the practice machine installed in this small device, where you can experiment with what you have learned. As an old saying goes, "practice makes perfect".

Responsive

You will not only learn from the provided courses, there will be questions and quizzes to help you solidify your knowledge and understandings.

All Browser Compatibility

You can access the content with all end devices, from mobile phones, tablets, and personal computers. Remember, you can always look again the content as many times as you want until you fully grasp the concepts.
